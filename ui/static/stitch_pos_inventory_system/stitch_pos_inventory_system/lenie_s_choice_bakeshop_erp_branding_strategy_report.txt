Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Needs Assessment Report
Lenie’s Choice Bakeshop

1. INTRODUCTION
ERP systems integrate various business functions into a single, centralized system. This report assesses whether Lenie’s Choice Bakeshop, a multi-branch bakery enterprise, requires an ERP system.

2. COMPANY BACKGROUND
Enterprise Name: Lenie’s Choice Bakeshop
Business Size: Small enterprise
Branches: Seven (7) branches in Cebu (Barili, Alegria, Malabuyoc, Ginatilan, Samboan).

3. CURRENT OPERATIONAL PROBLEMS
- Inventory Inaccuracies
- Manual Sales Recording
- Inefficient Procurement
- Poor Inter-Branch Coordination
- Human Error and Operational Delays

4. ERP FIT ANALYSIS
The enterprise needs an ERP to integrate inventory, sales, procurement, and financial data across its 7 branches.

5. ADVANTAGES
- Improved efficiency through automation.
- Real-time visibility across branches.
- Reduced administrative workload.

6. RECOMMENDATION
Phased modular implementation starting with Inventory, POS, and Procurement.
